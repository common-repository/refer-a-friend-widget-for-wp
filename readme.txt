== Installation ==

1.  Download the plugin to your computer, then log in to your WP admin area and go to Plugins > Add New. Browse to the plugin archive and select it. Then click Install Now and the plugin will be installed shortly.

2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

3. Visit <a href="https://invitebox.com/pricing" title="InviteBox website">InviteBox website</a> and sign up for our 14 day free trial or the plan that suits you best.

4. Log in to your InviteBox Dashboard at InviteBox.com and create your referral campaign, set the rules, and setup your InviteBox widget.

5. Copy your widget 'Secret key' from the 'API settings' section on the "Integration" page in your InviteBox Dashboard.

6. Go to the Wordpress Invitebox admin page. Click on the "Add New Campaign" button, and insert copied "Secret key" into the "Campaign Secret Key" field.

7. You can add the InviteBox widget on one page only or certain pages by adding the widget script to the page/post content. To show the InviteBox widget on all pages select "Default Campaign for all pages".

8. To track conversions on WooCommerce purchase go to WooCommerce->Settings->Invitebox and select campaigns.
